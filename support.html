Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Open your iPhone Settings → tap your name at the top → Subscriptions → select No Coast → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro features will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

How do I restore my purchase on a new device?

Open No Coast → tap the Settings gear icon → tap "Restore Purchases." Make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you used for the original purchase.

How do I change my check-in times?

Tap the Settings gear icon on the Today screen. From there you can add, remove, or change the times your check-ins are delivered.

I'm not receiving notifications

Make sure notifications are enabled for No Coast in your iPhone Settings → Notifications → No Coast. Also check that Do Not Disturb or a Focus mode isn't blocking them. NoCoast relies on notifications to deliver check-ins — without them, the app can't do its job.

How do I request a refund?

All purchases are processed by Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and follow Apple's refund process.

How do I delete my data?

All your data is stored locally on your device. Deleting the No Coast app removes everything. We don't store any personal data on our servers.
