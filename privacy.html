Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 16, 2026

No Coast ("the App") is operated by Seven Notes Private Limited ("we", "us", "our"). This privacy policy explains how we handle information when you use our iOS application.

Information We Collect

No Coast is designed with privacy in mind. The vast majority of your data stays on your device and is never sent to our servers.

Data stored locally on your device only: Your Big Thing (personal goal), check-in messages, notification schedule, check-in responses ("pumped" or "autopilot"), streak data, and custom check-ins. This data is stored using your device's local storage and is not transmitted to us or any third party.

Subscription data: If you subscribe to No Coast Pro, your purchase is processed by Apple through the App Store. We use RevenueCat, a third-party service, to manage subscription status. RevenueCat receives an anonymous identifier linked to your App Store purchase to verify your subscription. We do not receive your name, email, payment method, or Apple ID.

Anonymous analytics: We use PostHog to collect anonymous usage data to help us improve the app. This includes events like onboarding completion, check-in responses (without message content), and feature usage. No personally identifiable information is collected. You cannot be identified from this data.

Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, location, contacts, photos, browsing history, or any other personally identifiable information. We do not require you to create an account or sign in.

How We Use Information

Subscription data is used solely to verify your Pro subscription status and provide you with the features you've paid for. Anonymous analytics data is used to understand how people use the app so we can make it better.

Third-Party Services

RevenueCat: Manages subscription verification. Their privacy policy is available at revenuecat.com/privacy.

PostHog: Provides anonymous analytics. Their privacy policy is available at posthog.com/privacy.

Apple: Processes all payments through the App Store. Apple's privacy policy is available at apple.com/legal/privacy.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with anyone. We do not display ads. We do not use your data for marketing purposes.

Data Retention and Deletion

Since your data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by deleting the app. Uninstalling No Coast removes all locally stored data. Anonymous analytics data is retained for up to 12 months and cannot be linked back to you.

Children's Privacy

No Coast is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page and updating the effective date.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy, contact us at hello@sevennotesapps.com.
